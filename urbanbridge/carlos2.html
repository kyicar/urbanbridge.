Transportation - Bus stops ≤ 400 m apart in general urban areas (UN-Habitat).
- Stops every 200 m in high-density areas.
- Areas with ≥4,000 people/km² require frequent service.
- Aim for 2.5 bus stops/km² and 1.6 km of transit line/km² (Transport Research Laboratory). 		NASA Earthdata

Healthcare Facilities - ≥1 hospital per 100,000 people (WHO).
- Around 3–6 hospital beds per 1,000 people (World Bank).
- Access healthcare within 30 minutes by walking/public transport (WHO Universal Health Coverage). 		NASA Earth Observatory

Green Space - Minimum: 9 m²/person (WHO).
- Ideal: 50 m²/person.
- Everyone should live within a 5-minute walk of a green area.
- 20–30% of city land should be green/open space (UN-Habitat). 		NASA Earth Observatory
